Our Story


Waffle and Peace, nestled in the heart of Thrissur, is where passion for waffles meets a commitment to quality and hospitality. Our mission is simple: to craft waffle creations that delight the senses and create memorable moments for our guests.

At Waffle Delight, each waffle is a masterpiece, carefully crafted with the finest ingredients and a touch of culinary ingenuity. From classic favorites to innovative combinations, every bite is a symphony of flavors that leaves a lasting impression.

Step into our inviting space and experience the warmth of our hospitality. Whether you're gathering with friends, enjoying a solo treat, or hosting a special event, our dedicated team is here to ensure your experience at Waffle Delight is nothing short of exceptional.
